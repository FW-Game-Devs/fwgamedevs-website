Did you know that people in Fort Wayne make games?

Come out to Fort Cade to meet these people and play their games. Fort Cade is open June 13th from Noon to 5pm at the Downtown Allen County Public Library. Whether you make games yourself, are interested in getting started in game development, or just like playing games; we’d love to see you at Fort Cade!
