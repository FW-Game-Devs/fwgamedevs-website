FW Game Devs Games Showcase 2026!
Fort Cade 2026!
June 13th, 2026, Noon - 5PM
Allen County Public Library, Downtown Branch
900 Library Plaza, Fort Wayne, IN 46802
Did you know people in Fort Wayne make games?!

Come out to Fort Cade to meet these people and play their games. Fort Cade is open from Noon to 5pm at the Downtown Allen County Public Library. The showcase floor, filled with games to play, will be in Meeting Rooms A and B. We’ll also be hosting hourly talks and presentations about game development from local developers and teachers in Meeting Room C.

Whether you make games yourself, are interested in getting started in game development, or just like playing games; we’d love to see you at Fort Cade!

If you'd like to submit your game to be considered for the showcase, please fill out this Submission Form before Midnight on May 1st.

We're looking for playable games (full, demo, or vertical slice) from Indiana or the Midwest region of the US. If you're a developer who's excited to show their game to an eager audience and meet other developers in the region, we'd love to see your submission!
