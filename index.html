FW Game Devs Games Showcase 2026!
We are the Fort Wayne Game Devs meetup group. We hang out on our Discord Server, mingle in public for monthly general meetups where we playtest each other's games, and we do weekly coworking sessions that we coordinate through the Discord server.
On May 9th, 2026, we'll be hosting our first Video Game Showcase where we'll be highlighting local and regional indie games for the Fort Wayne public to play.

If you'd like to submit your game to be considered for the showcase, check back here on February 23rd for a form link. We're looking for playable games (full, demo, or vertical slice) from Indiana or the Midwest region of the US. If you're a developer who's excited to show their game to an eager audience and meet other developers in the region, we'd love to see your submission!
